Experience with Technology

My interest in Computers started with my dad. He has years of experience doing a variety of Computer Science oriented jobs and has taught me a lot of what I know. In High School, I learned three different coding languages from different classes: HTML, Visual Basic and Java Script. I learned HTML from an after school coding class I took in 9th grade, and I learned Visual Basic and Javascript from Computer Science classes I took. I then joined a pilot class called Cyber Security in 12th grade. We were tasked with completing labs on securing systems to prevent hackers from accessing it. Overall, these experiences have made me develop an interest in the field and I now wish to pursue it as a career! I am currently enrolled at Mount Allison University to get a Degree in Science with a major of Computer Science

Cyber Titan

In 2019, I joined a competition with a team of 5 other people. We were in the Cyber Titans cybersecurity competition held in Ottawa after winning top 10 in Canada! At the competition, we got a bunch of input from people who have years of experience in the Cyber Security field

Drama

Starting in 2016, I have been in the Moncton High School Drama department ever since. I started out by doing props and sets, bringing out needed props in between each scene.
